Hello!

...and welcome to Get Stuffed catering.

We are a young company with a passion for fine food, a relaxed environment, and a good show. Whether it’s breakfast, brunch, lunch, tea, canapés, dinner or a midnight snack, Get Stuffed can provide.

We make, bake, butcher and smoke what we can from scratch, and always do it with excitement and imagination. Our suppliers are our friends, and our charming chefs will be yours.

We cut our teeth in some of the best kitchens around the world. We have catered for weddings, private parties, corporate events and films and impressed high profile clients including Nicolas Cage, Hayden Christenson, Hugh Fearnley-Whittingstall and Brian Capron.

Email us at getstuffedcateringco@gmail.com to find out more
