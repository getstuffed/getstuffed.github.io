Hello

We are Get Stuffed Catering, a collective of chefs passionate about creating beautiful food with attitude, imagination and style.

We make, bake, butcher and smoke what we can from scratch, and do it with excitement and verve. Our suppliers are our friends, and our charming chefs will be yours.

We create memorable and unique food experiences for press events, photo & film shoots, corporate lunches, parties, and pop-ups.

Email us at info@getstuffedcatering.co.uk or come join us for a taste and a chat at our beautiful studio kitchen in SE5

Yours for food,

Chris & Henry
